Mekk-Knight

Mastering the Columns of Light

Executive Summary

The Mekk-Knight archetype offers a unique, reactive playstyle centered on column-based gameplay. As key figures in the "World Legacy" lore, they manifest on the field by capitalizing on the opponent's board presence, making them a premier "go-second" strategy designed to break established fields. Their strength lies in turning the game board itself into a puzzle, forcing opponents to reconsider every card placement lest they create the very columns of light from which these powerful knights emerge to generate overwhelming advantage.

The Main Deck Knights

Mekk-Knight Blue Sky

Blue Sky

The primary advantage engine. When summoned, it searches for "Mekk-Knight" monsters equal to the number of opponent's cards in its column, making it devastating against an established board.

Mekk-Knight Purple Nightfall

Purple Nightfall

The most versatile knight. Its Quick Effect banishes a Mekk-Knight (usually itself) to search another, dodging effects and ensuring a constant flow of resources.

Mekk-Knight Indigo Eclipse

Indigo Eclipse

The core of the control strategy. Its Quick Effect moves a Mekk-Knight to another column, enabling mobile negation when paired with "World Legacy's Secret".

Girsu, the Orcust Mekk-Knight

Girsu, the Orcust

The deck's go-first enabler. It creates a column by summoning tokens and sends a "World Legacy" card to the GY, setting up powerful Extra Deck plays.

Core Combo: The Girsu Lockdown

When forced to go first, Mekk-Knights rely on Girsu to establish a formidable control board from a single card.

1

Normal Summon Girsu, the Orcust Mekk-Knight. Use its effect to send a "World Legacy" card to the GY and then summon tokens to both fields, creating a column.

2

Link Summon Lib the World Key Blademaster using Girsu and your token. Lib's effect activates, setting World Legacy's Secret directly from your Deck.

3

Special Summon Mekk-Knight Purple Nightfall to the column with the opponent's token, then use its effect to search for Mekk-Knight Indigo Eclipse.

The Established End Board

This setup provides a powerful, movable monster effect negation that can control the entire flow of the opponent's turn.

Competitive Analysis

Strengths

  • Exceptional board-breaking power when going second.
  • Potent resource loop between Blue Sky and Purple Nightfall.
  • High skill ceiling that rewards strategic card placement.

Weaknesses

  • Go-first inconsistency without access to Girsu.
  • Vulnerable to backrow removal like Harpie's Feather Duster.
  • Relies on opponent's board, can struggle against empty fields.

Key Support

  • Lib the World Key Blademaster for consistent access to control cards.
  • Mekk-Knight Crusadia Avramax as an indestructible boss monster.
  • Invoked & Orcust engines synergize well with the deck's attributes and types.
