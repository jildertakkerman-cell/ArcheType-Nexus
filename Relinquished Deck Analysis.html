Relinquished

The Art of Absorption

Executive Summary

The Relinquished archetype is a classic control strategy focused on a unique parasitic mechanic: absorbing an opponent's monsters and turning their power against them. This playstyle is executed through a small but potent lineup of strange and powerful entities, primarily Ritual and Fusion monsters known as "Eyes Restrict". The deck excels at non-destructive removal, bypassing many forms of protection, and thrives in a "go-second" environment where it can dismantle established boards.

The Pantheon of Eyes: Core Monsters

Relinquished

The Ritual foundation. Absorbs one monster to steal its stats. While outdated as a boss, it is the essential search target for Pre-Preparation of Rites, making the entire engine consistent.

Thousand-Eyes Restrict

The proactive board-breaker. After absorbing a monster, it prevents all other monsters from attacking or changing position. A prime target for Instant Fusion.

Millennium-Eyes Restrict

The reactive disruption. A Quick Effect allows it to absorb an opponent's monster when they activate an effect, negating it and all other copies of that monster. The deck's primary interactive piece.

Relinquished Anima

The Link-1 tool. Absorbs any monster it points to. Its main purpose is to enable the "Kaiju Slingshot" combo, turning a Kaiju given to the opponent into your own high-ATK monster.

Competitive Analysis

Strengths

  • Unconventional Removal: Kaiju tributing and non-destructive absorption bypass most monster protection.
  • Potent Disruption: Millennium-Eyes Restrict can shut down key monster effects and hand traps.
  • Go-Second Power: Excels at breaking established boards rather than building its own.

Weaknesses

  • Inconsistency: Relies on drawing specific starter cards or two-card combos to function.
  • Weak Going First: Struggles to establish a meaningful board without external engines like Invoked.
  • Backrow Vulnerability: Has few in-archetype answers to powerful Spells, Traps, and floodgates.

Tech & Engines

  • Kaiju Engine: Essential for consistent board-breaking and enabling Anima.
  • Pre-Preparation of Rites: The deck's best starter, providing a +1 in card advantage.
  • Super Polymerization: Another powerful, un-chainable removal tool against DARK-heavy metas.

Current TCG Banlist Status

Limited Cards (1 per Deck)

Unrestricted Cards

  • All "Relinquished" and "Eyes Restrict" monsters
  • All in-archetype support cards

Meta Implications: The archetype itself remains at full power. The limitation of Instant Fusion slightly reduces the deck's explosive opening potential but does not cripple its core strategy, which primarily relies on its Ritual and dedicated Fusion spells.
