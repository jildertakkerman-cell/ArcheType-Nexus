DORIADO

Analysis of the Elemental Lineage

Executive Summary

The "Doriado" series is not a conventional archetype, but a thematic lineage of monsters united by a singular concept: mastery over the elemental Attributes. This collection—a Ritual monster, a Pendulum monster, and a powerful Nomi monster—forms the basis for unique and challenging strategies. Their power is not in isolation but in their synergistic potential with each other and a vast pool of generic support that enables their demanding mechanics.

The Elemental Conclave

Elemental Mistress Doriado

The elemental nexus. As a Ritual Monster, her continuous effect treats her as WIND, WATER, FIRE, and EARTH simultaneously, enabling powerful support cards that require multiple attributes on the field.

Dark Doriado

The pendulum oracle. Its monster effect allows you to stack the top of your deck with one monster of each of the four main elements, giving you unparalleled control over your future draws.

Elemental Grace Doriado

The ascended matriarch. This boss monster requires 6+ different Attributes in the graveyards to be summoned and can negate opponent's summons multiple times in a turn.

Blueprint: Mistress Control

This strategy focuses on summoning Elemental Mistress Doriado to enable the devastating trap card, Fuh-Rin-Ka-Zan, which mimics four of the most powerful banned spell cards in the game's history.

Competitive Analysis

Strengths

  • Element of Surprise: As a rogue strategy, opponents are unlikely to know your choke points.
  • High-Impact Payoffs: A resolved Fuh-Rin-Ka-Zan or Elemental Grace Doriado can win the game on the spot.
  • Engine Compatibility: Can be integrated with various powerful generic engines (Ritual, Sky Striker, Lightsworn).

Weaknesses

  • Normal Summon Reliance: Key combos often depend on a single Normal Summon, making them vulnerable to hand traps.
  • Graveyard Dependency: The Grace Turbo strategy is completely shut down by Graveyard-hate like D.D. Crow or Dimension Shifter.
  • Fragile Boards: The Mistress Control setup is easily dismantled by simple Spell/Trap removal or monster negation.

Key Synergies

  • Sky Striker Engine: The most efficient way to summon Elemental Grace Doriado, providing all 6 attributes from a one-card starter.
  • Ritual Support: Generic searchers like Manju and Preparation of Rites are mandatory for the Mistress Control variant.
  • Elementsaber Engine: A slower but thematically appropriate partner for filling the Graveyard with attributes over time.

Current TCG Banlist Status

FULLY UNRESTRICTED: The entire core "Doriado" archetype and its direct support cards are not on the Forbidden & Limited list.

Core Cards (3 copies each)

  • Elemental Mistress Doriado
  • Dark Doriado
  • Elemental Grace Doriado
  • Doriado's Blessing
  • Fuh-Rin-Ka-Zan

Engine Considerations

  • Key Ritual searchers are unrestricted.
  • Some powerful generic engines (e.g., Sky Striker) may have limited cards.
  • The deck's power comes from combining unlimited cards in unique ways.

Meta Implications: As a fully unrestricted rogue strategy, Doriado's strength is its creativity. While its core is at full power, its competitive viability depends on the player's skill in piloting it and adapting its build with other powerful, generic cards that may be on the banlist.
