CLEAR WORLD

The Art of Attribute Warfare

Executive Summary

The Clear World archetype is a methodical control strategy that weaponizes the Attributes of monsters. By using its Field Spell, "Clear World", the deck imposes severe penalties on the opponent based on the Attributes they control. The archetype's own "Clear" monsters are immune to these effects, creating a one-sided floodgate. The goal is to establish this lockdown, slowly grind the opponent out of resources, and achieve victory through strategic attrition.

Clear World

Clear Wall

Competitive Analysis

Strengths

  • Potent Floodgate: The full lock is extremely difficult for many decks to play through.
  • Consistent Setup: The "Clear Phantom" combo provides a reliable path to the main strategy.
  • Hybrid Potential: Synergizes well with engines that appreciate discard costs or provide tribute fodder.

Weaknesses

  • Single Choke Point: The entire combo is vulnerable to a single hand trap on "Clear Phantom".
  • Spell/Trap Removal: The lock crumbles if "Clear World" or "Clear Wall" is removed.
  • Poor Recovery: A broken board is very difficult to re-establish for a pure build.

Synergistic Engines

  • Danger!: Turns discard costs into free Special Summons to accelerate combos.
  • Horus: Provides a resilient, self-reviving engine for consistent tribute fodder.
  • Kashtira: Offers free Level 7 bodies to instantly enable "Clear Wall's" full lock.
