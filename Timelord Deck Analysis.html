TIMELORD

Masters of Stall, Burn, and Inevitability

Executive Summary

The Timelord archetype is a unique control strategy that operates on a completely different axis from most modern decks. Instead of building complex boards, Timelords deploy a single, powerful monster per turn that is immune to destruction and prevents the player from taking battle damage. Their goal is to systematically dismantle the opponent's board with disruptive effects that activate after battling, all while using burn damage to secure victory. The deck is fundamentally reactive, designed to punish over-extension and out-grind opponents through sheer resilience.

The Celestial Roster & Support

Key Figures

Time Maiden

The heart of the machine. A one-card starter that searches any 0 ATK Timelord and enables Rank 10 plays from the Graveyard.

Metaion, the Timelord

The premier board-clear for monsters, returning all other monsters on the field to the hand after battling.

Michion, the Timelord

The great equalizer and core of the OTK strategy. Its effect halves the opponent's Life Points.

The Path to Infinity

The deck's primary lockdown is achieved through a chain of Continuous Traps that transform the temporary Timelords into permanent, untargetable threats. This sequence is the archetype's ultimate win condition.

Empty Machine → Infinite Machine → Infinite Light

This progression allows you to Special Summon Timelords from your hand and ultimately makes them permanent, untargetable, and indestructible threats on the field.

The full power of the celestial roster of angels lies in choosing the right tool for any situation.

With no monsters on your field, Special Summon Time Maiden from your hand. Then, Tribute her to search for the perfect Timelord to counter your opponent's board, such as Kamion, the Timelord.

Competitive Analysis

Strengths

  • Inherent Resilience: Immunity to destruction by battle and card effects forces opponents to use non-standard removal.
  • Non-Targeting Removal: Many Timelords shuffle cards into the deck without targeting, bypassing common protection effects.
  • Explosive OTK: The Rank 10 "Train" engine can end the game unexpectedly out of a seemingly passive board state.

Weaknesses

  • Effect Negation: A negated Timelord becomes a 0 ATK monster that is easily destroyed by battle.
  • Battle Phase Dependency: The entire strategy crumbles if the Battle Phase is skipped or attacks are negated.
  • Non-Destruction Removal: Highly vulnerable to being banished, returned to the hand/deck, or Tributed by cards like Kaijus.

Key Synergies

  • Kaiju Engine: The perfect partnership. Tribute an opponent's indestructible boss monster and attack the Kaiju with Raphion for massive burn damage.
  • Generic Burn/Stall: Floodgates like There Can Be Only One and burn cards like Magic Cylinder supplement the core strategy.

Current TCG Banlist Status

UNRESTRICTED STATUS: The Timelord archetype and its core support cards are currently at full power in the TCG, with no cards on the Forbidden & Limited list. This allows the deck to operate with maximum consistency and access all its powerful stall and OTK strategies.
