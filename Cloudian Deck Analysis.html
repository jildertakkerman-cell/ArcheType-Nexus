Cloudian

An Analysis of the Fog Counter Swarm Engine

Executive Summary

The Cloudian archetype is a unique strategy centered around "Fog Counters" and swarming the field with low-level monsters. While they are immune to battle destruction, they have a critical weakness: they are destroyed if they are in face-up Defense Position. Modern Cloudian decks have pivoted from a slow control strategy to a fast-paced combo deck that uses its monsters as material for powerful, generic Link and Xyz summons. Their whimsical, weather-based designs hide a surprisingly explosive potential, as explored in their lore.

Foundational Mechanics: The Eye of the Storm

Fog Counters

The core resource of the archetype. Fog Counters are placed on Cloudian monsters and can be removed to activate various effects, from destroying cards to swarming the field with tokens.

Link & Xyz Swarm

The modern strategy focuses on using Cloudian - Turbulence to summon multiple Cloudian - Smoke Balls, which are then used as material for powerful Extra Deck monsters.

Core Combo: The Perfect Storm

The main combo uses Cloudian - Turbulence and Cloudian Aerosol to quickly fill your board and set up your end board.

Competitive Analysis

Strengths

  • Explosive Swarm: Can easily fill the board with monsters from a one-card starter.
  • Powerful End Board: Capable of creating multiple negates and disruptions.
  • Good Recursion: Can recycle its resources for follow-up plays.

Weaknesses

  • Normal Summon Reliant: Very vulnerable to hand traps that negate the first summon.
  • Fragile Combo: The main combo is linear and can be easily disrupted.
  • Lack of Protection: Cloudian monsters are easily removed by card effects.

Key Tech & Synergies

  • WATER Extenders: Cards like Silent Angler help make your Xyz plays more consistent.
  • Fairy Support: Archlord Kristya can be a powerful floodgate that shuts down your opponent.
  • Generic WATER support: Cards like Salvage can help you recover resources for a grind game.
