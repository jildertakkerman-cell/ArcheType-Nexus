NEXUS

Memento

A Symphony of Self-Destruction and Creation

Executive Summary

The Memento archetype operates on a seemingly paradoxical principle: strategic self-destruction as the primary engine for creation. Unlike archetypes where destroying one's own cards is a cost, for Memento, it is the central mechanic that converts on-field and in-hand resources into board presence, graveyard setup, and ultimately, overwhelming advantage. Understanding this core philosophy is the first step to mastering its complex and non-linear gameplay. This process populates the Graveyard with five "Memento" monsters of different names, setting the stage for the deck's ultimate boss monster.

The Cast of Characters: Core Cards

The Memento archetype is composed of a diverse cast, each with a specific role. Understanding these roles—Starter, Extender, Payoff, or Utility—is essential for navigating the deck's intricate decision trees.

The Cast: Core Monsters

Mementotlan Angwitch

The premier starter. On summon, adds any "Memento" monster from Deck to hand.

Mementotlan Dark Blade

Destroys a "Memento" to Special Summon another from the Deck.

Mementotlan Tatsunootoshigo

Destroys a "Memento" to send others from Deck to GY, setting up combos.

Mementotlan Shleepy

Summons itself from hand when a Memento is destroyed, enabling a Fusion Summon.

Mementoal Tecuhtlica - Combined Creation

The main boss. 5000 ATK, attacks all monsters, and revives Mementos from the GY as a Quick Effect.

The Script: Spells & Traps

Mementotlan Fusion

Quick-Play Fusion. Its GY effect banishes itself to destroy a Memento and search any Memento S/T.

Mementotlan Bone Party

Quick-Play extender. Destroys a Memento from hand/field to search or summon another from Deck.

Mementomictlan

The Field Spell. Revives a Memento on destruction and recycles a Memento S/T from GY in the End Phase.

Mementotlan Cranium Burst

Continuous Trap that provides multiple monster effect negations by targeting your "Combined Creation".

Core Combo: The Tatsunootoshigo Opening

This walkthrough demonstrates how one card, Mementotlan Tatsunootoshigo, can generate a full board by initiating the deck's cascade of self-destruction and revival.

1

The Opening Mill

Special Summon Tatsunootoshigo. Activate its effect, destroying itself to send Mace (Lvl 1), Goblin (Lvl 1), and Ghattic (Lvl 3) from Deck to GY. Ghattic triggers, reviving itself, then adds Mace from GY to hand.

2

First Fusion Summon

Normal Summon Mace. Activate its effect, destroying itself to search Mementotlan Fusion. Activate Fusion, shuffling Mace, Tatsu, and Ghattic from GY to summon Creation King.

3

Mill & Search Field Spell

Chain Link 1: Creation King (on-field) sends Angwitch, a 2nd Goblin, and a 2nd King to GY. Chain Link 2: King (2) (in GY) banishes itself to search the Field Spell, Mementomictlan. Activate it.

4

Cascade Trigger

Activate Fusion's GY effect, banishing it and destroying Creation King to search Bone Party. The Field Spell triggers, reviving Angwitch. Angwitch then triggers, searching Shleepy.

5

Second Fusion Summon

Shleepy summons itself from hand. Activate Shleepy's on-field effect, fusing itself (from field) and other Mementos (from GY) to summon Mementotlan Twin Dragon.

6

Goblin Mill & Extenders

Twin Dragon pops itself, searching Horned Dragon & Combined Creation. Twin Dragon's GY effect triggers, reviving Goblin (2). Goblin pops itself to send 2 more names (like Dark Blade) to GY.

7

S:P Little Knight

Activate Bone Party, destroying Angwitch to revive Mace from GY. Special Summon Horned Dragon from hand (since 3+ Mementos are in GY). Link Summon S:P Little Knight using Mace and Horned Dragon.

8

The Final Board

With S:P Little Knight on field, Special Summon Combined Creation by shuffling 5 Mementos (Tatsu, Angwitch, Twin Dragon, etc.) from GY to Deck. In the End Phase, the Field Spell sets Bone Party from your GY.

The Final Masterpiece: A Typical Endboard

Anatomy of a Memento Board

A powerful Memento endboard is a multi-faceted web of layered interactions designed to systematically dismantle an opponent's turn. It's not just a wall of negates, but a diverse set of disruptions.

  • The Fortress: Combined Creation on field.
  • The Negation: Mementotlan Cranium Burst to provide multiple monster negates.
  • The Removal: Creation King in GY, ready to be revived for destruction.
  • The Hand Trap: Mementotlan Mace in hand to steal an opponent's monster.
  • The Follow-up: Link support like S:P Little Knight for non-destruction removal.

Competitive Analysis

Strengths

  • High Power Ceiling: Capable of producing endboards with over eight distinct points of layered disruption.
  • Resilience: With expert piloting, the deck can navigate multiple hand traps and interruptions using its Quick-Play Spells.
  • OTK Potential: Akihiron enables Combined Creation to attack directly for 5000 damage, often leading to a swift victory.

Weaknesses

  • Complexity & Timer: Long, intricate combos are difficult to master and can lead to losses against the in-game timer.
  • Graveyard Dependency: Fundamentally reliant on the GY and can be severely hampered by floodgates like Dimension Shifter.
  • Vulnerability to Maxx "C": Performs a high number of Special Summons, making an unanswered Maxx "C" a near-certain loss.

External Engines

  • Fiendsmith Engine: Provides additional 1-card starters and raises the deck's power ceiling.
  • Goblin Biker Engine: Offers defensive utility, allowing you to play through disruption by Tributing targeted monsters.

Card Overview: The Memento Arsenal

A detailed breakdown of the archetype's most critical components and their roles in your strategy.

Mementotlan Angwitch

Monster / Starter

The best starter. On summon, searches any "Memento" monster. Also destroys a Memento to revive a Level 2 or lower Memento from the GY.

Mementotlan Dark Blade

Monster / Starter

A potent starter that destroys a Memento (including itself) to Special Summon a Level 3 or lower Memento directly from the Deck.

Mementotlan Tatsunootoshigo

Monster / Extender

The main mill engine. Destroys a Memento to send other Mementos from Deck to GY whose total Levels equal the destroyed monster's Level.

Mementotlan Shleepy

Monster / Extender

Critical combo piece. Summons itself from hand when a Memento is destroyed, then immediately enables a Fusion Summon.

Mementoal Tecuhtlica - Combined Creation

Monster / Payoff

The 5000 ATK main boss. Attacks all monsters and has a Quick Effect to destroy a Memento to revive any Memento from the GY.

Mementomictlan Tecuhtlica - Creation King

Monster / Payoff

Fusion boss that mills 3 on summon and has a Quick Effect to trade your Mementos for opponent's cards via destruction.

Mementotlan Fusion

Spell / Utility

Quick-Play Fusion Spell. Its crucial GY effect lets you banish it and destroy a Memento to search any Memento Spell/Trap.

Mementotlan Bone Party

Spell / Extender

A vital Quick-Play extender that destroys a Memento from hand/field to summon or search another Memento from the Deck.

Mementomictlan

Spell / Utility

The Field Spell. Protects battling Mementos and revives a Memento from hand/GY when one of your monsters is destroyed.

Mementotlan Cranium Burst

Trap / Payoff

Continuous Trap that provides multiple monster negates per turn by targeting your "Combined Creation".
