Madolche

A Confectioner's Compendium to the Sweetest Archetype

The Madolche Kingdom

The Madolche archetype operates on a unique and resilient core philosophy. Centered around EARTH monsters with dessert and royalty motifs, the deck facilitates a mid-range playstyle that excels at resource management, board protection, and explosive, game-ending turns. Its fundamental mechanics ensure that its resources are rarely depleted, making it a formidable opponent in protracted duels.

The defining characteristic is the "No Pastries in the Trash" recursion: when a Main Deck Madolche monster is destroyed by an opponent and sent to the Graveyard (GY), its effect triggers to shuffle itself back into the Main Deck. This establishes the deck's identity of persistence, allowing it to maintain key combo pieces and grind out advantages over time with a royal court of sweet treats that never truly leave the field.

The Royal Court: Core Cards

The Initiators (Starters)

  • Madolche Petingcessoeur: The premier starter. Summons herself from hand if your GY is empty, then summons another Madolche from the Deck.
  • Madolche Anjelly: Tributes herself to summon any Madolche from the Deck, providing fuel for Hootcake.
  • Madolche Magileine: A reliable Normal Summon that searches any Madolche monster from Deck to hand.

The Connectors (Extenders)

  • Madolche Hootcake: Banishes a monster from any GY to summon a Madolche from the Deck. A crucial mid-combo piece.
  • Madolche Messengelato: Searches any Madolche Spell/Trap when Special Summoned while you control a Beast-type Madolche.

The Monarchy (Win Conditions)

  • Madolche Queen Tiaramisu: The iconic boss monster. Shuffles your Madolche cards from GY to shuffle opponent's cards into the Deck (non-targeting).
  • Madolche Puddingcess Chocolat-a-la-Mode: The OTK enabler. Summons Madolches from the Deck when a Madolche is returned to the deck (not once per turn).

Core Combo: The One-Card Starter

This walkthrough shows how one card, such as Madolche Anjelly, can lead to a full board with multiple interruptions.

Madolche Anjelly

1. Normal Summon Anjelly. Tribute her to Special Summon Petingcessoeur from Deck. Petingcessoeur's effect then Special Summons Puddingcess.

Madolche Teacher Glassouffle

2. Xyz Summon Glassouffle. Use her effect, detaching a material to put a Madolche in the GY. Then, Xyz Summon Chocolat-a-la-Mode using Glassouffle.

Madolche Puddingcess Chocolat-a-la-Mode

3. Trigger Chocolat-a-la-Mode. Her effect shuffles the Madolche from the GY into the Deck, which then triggers her other effect to Special Summon Hootcake. Hootcake banishes Anjelly from GY to summon Messengelato, which searches Madolche Salon.

The Final Presentation: End Boards

Going-First Fortress

A standard go-first board establishes a recurring loop of disruption.

Madolche Teacher Glassouffle
Madolche Fresh Sistart

Key Interruptions:

  • Madolche Promenade (Set): Searchable omni-negate.
  • Glassouffle: GY disruption and monster effect protection.
  • Sistart: Protects Field Spell from targeting.
  • Salon + Ticket Loop: Recycles resources and sets up follow-up plays.

Going-Second Assault (OTK)

Focuses on breaking the opponent's board and winning in a single turn.

Madolche Queen Tiaramisu
Madolche Puddingcess Chocolat-a-la-Mode

Key Plays:

  • Tiaramisu: Non-targeting removal to clear two threats.
  • Chocolat-a-la-Mode: Swarms the field with monsters from the deck.
  • Chateau ATK Boost: A 500 ATK boost pushes monsters into lethal range. A typical board can easily reach over 8000 damage.
