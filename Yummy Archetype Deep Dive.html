NEXUS

Yummy

A Deep Dive into the Sweet and Synergistic Archetype

Executive Summary

The YUMMY archetype is a highly-synergistic, combo-centric strategy centered on LIGHT Beast monsters. The deck's primary mode of operation revolves around a continuous loop of Special Summons, leveraging its Level 1 Main Deck monsters and a key Link-1 monster to repeatedly access its powerful Level 2 Synchro monsters. This recursive engine allows the deck to generate significant card advantage and deploy reactive disruption. Their delightful appearance hides a surprisingly complex strategy, a recipe for victory some might say.

The Main Ingredients: Core Cards

The Level 1 Swarmers

Cupsy☆Yummy

The primary starter. When summoned, searches for any other YUMMY card from the Deck.

Cooky☆Yummy

A versatile extender that can reduce an opponent's monster's ATK or destroy it if Special Summoned by a Synchro.

Lollipo☆Yummy

Specializes in graveyard disruption, either shuffling a card back into the deck or banishing it.

The Engine and Enablers

Yummy★Snatchy (Link-1)

The central hub of the archetype. Places a YUMMY Field Spell from the Deck and enables Quick-Synchro plays.

YUMMY "Way" Synchros (Level 2)

The core looping engine. They provide advantage on summon and can "tag-out" to revive monsters from the GY as a Quick Effect.

Yummyusment☆Mignon (Field Spell)

Provides recursion from the GY and a massive ATK boost to all YUMMY monsters, enabling OTKs.

The Recipe for Victory: Combo Guides

The YUMMY deck is famous for its consistency, with multiple 1-card starters. Select a combo below to see how they establish a resilient board.

Combo: 1-Card "Yummy" Starter

This is the deck's most fundamental combo, turning any single "Yummy" monster into a recursive engine and multiple interruptions.

Cupsy☆Yummy
Step 1: Start by Normal Summoning Cupsy☆Yummy. Its CL1 effect activates, letting you add Lollipo☆Yummy from your deck to your hand.
Yummy★Snatchy
Step 2: Link away Cupsy☆ to summon your main combo piece, Yummy★Snatchy.
Yummyusment☆Mignon
Step 3: Snatchy's effect activates as CL1, placing the Yummyusment☆Mignon Field Spell from your Deck directly into your Field Zone.
Cupsy☆Yummy
Step 4: Now that Mignon is active, its CL1 effect triggers, Special Summoning Cupsy☆ back from your Graveyard.
Yummy☆Surprise
Step 5: Since Cupsy☆ was Special Summoned, its effect triggers again as CL1, this time adding the Yummy☆Surprise trap card.
Lollipo☆Yummy
Step 6: Special Summon the Lollipo☆Yummy you searched earlier. Its own effect lets you do this while you control a Link-1 monster (your Snatchy).
Cupsy★Yummy Way
Step 7: Use Snatchy's Quick Effect to perform a Synchro Summon. It treats itself as a L1 Tuner and uses your revived Cupsy☆ to make Cupsy★Yummy Way.
Cooky☆Yummy
Step 8: On summon, Cupsy★Way's CL1 effect lets you add two Cooky☆Yummy from deck to hand, then you discard one card.
Yummy☆Surprise
Step 9: Set the Yummy☆Surprise trap card you searched earlier to finish your turn.
Cupsy★Yummy Way
End Board:
Cupsy★Way + Lollipo☆ on field, Yummy☆Surprise set, Mignon active, and 2 Cooky☆ extenders in hand.

Combo: 1-Card "Obedience Schooled"

A "Nibiru-proof" line that establishes an omni-negate before the 5th summon by leveraging Martial Metal Marcher.

Obedience Schooled
Step 1: Activate Obedience Schooled. This summons three Level 1 Beasts from your deck (Cupsy☆, Cooky☆, Lollipo☆), but their effects are negated.
Yummy★Snatchy
Step 2: Link away the negated Cupsy☆ to summon your first Yummy★Snatchy.
Yummyusment☆Mignon

Cupsy☆Yummy
Step 4: The Field Spell's CL1 effect now triggers, reviving the Cupsy☆ from your GY. Since it's no longer on the field from 'Obedience Schooled', its effects are active again!
Yummy☆Surprise
Step 5: With its effects active, the revived Cupsy☆ triggers as CL1, adding Yummy☆Surprise to your hand.
Cupsy★Yummy Way
Step 6: Use Snatchy's Quick Effect, treating itself as a Tuner to Synchro Summon Cupsy★Yummy Way using the negated Cooky☆ as material.
Martial Metal Marcher
Step 7: Synchro Summon again! Use your revived Cupsy☆ (L1 Tuner) and your new Cupsy★Way (which is also a L2 Synchro Tuner) to make Martial Metal Marcher (L3).
Cupsy☆Yummy
Step 8: Marcher's CL1 effect activates on summon, reviving the Cupsy☆ (L1 Tuner) from your GY *again*.
Yummy★Snatchy
Step 9: Use the last monster from 'Obedience Schooled', Lollipo☆, to Link Summon a second Yummy★Snatchy.
Yummy☆Surprise
Step 10: Set the Yummy☆Surprise you searched to end the combo.
Herald of the Arc Light
End Board:
Snatchy (2), Marcher, & Cupsy☆ (Tuner). On your opponent's turn, you can use your second Snatchy's Quick Effect with Marcher to Synchro Summon Herald of the Arc Light (L4) for an omni-negate!

The Final Course: Endboard Analysis

The YUMMY deck's final board isn't a static wall of negates. Instead, it's a multi-layered, resilient, and recursive setup designed to adapt and out-grind the opponent. The goal is a dynamic combination of "soft" interruptions (dodging, reviving) and "hard" interruptions (negates, bounces) that allow the deck to maintain pressure and threaten an OTK on the follow-up.

Cupsy★Yummy Way

The Recursive Loop

The "Way" Synchros are the heart of the grind game. Their Quick Effect to "tag-out" (return to Extra Deck) to revive 2 YUMMYs from GY dodges removal and immediately refuels the board for Yummy★Snatchy to use again.

Herald of the Arc Light

The Omni-Negate

Optimal combos (especially with Obedience Schooled or Hornet Drones) aim to end on Herald of the Arc Light for a crucial omni-negate that also banishes cards sent from the hand or Deck to the GY.

Cooky★Yummy Way

Targeted Disruption

Cooky★YYummy Way provides a non-destructive "Book of Moon" effect, easily accessed via Snatchy on the opponent's turn to flip 2 monsters face-down.

Yummy☆Surprise

Flexible Interaction

A set Yummy☆Surprise is a multi-tool: it can bounce 2 opposing cards, Special Summon a YUMMY from GY to dodge an effect (like Dark Ruler No More), or recycle Mignon.

Competitive Analysis

Strengths

  • High Consistency: Can initiate combos with a wide variety of 1-card starters and external engines.
  • Resilience: The "tag-out" effect of the Synchros allows the deck to dodge targeting effects and rebuild its board.
  • OTK Potential: Can generate massive ATK boosts with its Field Spells for a swift victory.

Weaknesses

  • Hand Trap Vulnerability: While resilient, Ash Blossom on Obedience Schooled or Snatchy's *Ignition* effect can be painful.
  • Board Breakers: Super Polymerization (into Garura) or Dimensional Barrier (calling Synchro) can be devastating.
  • Floodgates: Light-Imprisoning Mirror or Power Filter shut down the entire engine.

External Engines & Tech

  • Sky Striker: Hornet Drones (Limited) and Kagari provide a powerful 1-card starter.
  • Sinful Spoils: A robust engine for searching and Special Summoning that adds recursion and bodies for Link plays.
  • Obedience Schooled (Limited): Mass Special Summons three beasts from the deck to instantly start combos.
