Shiranui

The Undying Spirit of the Synchro Saga

Philosophy of the Shiranui Clan

The Shiranui archetype is a FIRE Zombie-type theme centered on Synchro Summoning, defined by a unique and powerful core mechanic: generating advantage from the banish pile. Unlike many strategies where the Graveyard is the primary resource zone, Shiranui treats both the Graveyard and the Banish Pile as active extensions of the hand. This philosophy can be distilled into a central principle: "Banish & Benefit." The identity of these spectral samurai of the Shiranui clan is mechanically critical, granting them access to one of the most robust pools of generic Zombie support in the game.

The Shiranui Arsenal

Starters & Playmakers

Shiranui Solitaire

The deck's premier one-card starter. Tributes a Zombie to Special Summon a 0 DEF Zombie Tuner (like Uni-Zombie) from the Deck.

Shiranui Squire

A secondary starter. On Normal Summon, Special Summons a "Spectralsword" from hand or Deck, enabling an immediate Synchro or Link summon.

Shiranui Spiritmaster

On Normal Summon, revives a "Shiranui" from hand or GY. When banished, it destroys one face-up card your opponent controls.

The Spectral Swords

Shiranui Spectralsword

The heart of the engine. While in the GY, can banish itself and another Zombie to Synchro Summon a FIRE Zombie Synchro.

Shiranui Spectralsword Shade

A key recovery tool. Can Tribute itself from the field to Special Summon two banished Zombies (at least one "Shiranui").

Shiranui Style

Shiranui Style Swallow's Slash

A powerful trap. Tributes a Zombie to target and destroy 2 cards on the field, then banishes a "Shiranui" monster from the Deck, often triggering another effect.

Ghost Meets Girl - A Shiranui's Story

A potent extender. If your opponent controls a monster, you can discard a Zombie to Special Summon a "Shiranui" from your Deck or GY.
