Six Samurai

A Strategic Analysis of the Bushido Archetype

Executive Summary

The Six Samurai archetype is a venerable and complex Warrior-based strategy renowned for its capacity for explosive, game-ending turns. At its core, the deck's philosophy is built upon generating Bushido Counters and swarming the field with multiple monsters. What elevates this engine is a crucial design detail: key spell cards like Gateway of the Six lack a "hard once per turn" restriction, enabling the infamous infinite combos that define the archetype's identity among the many legendary warriors in the game.

The Roster of Warriors

The strength of the Six Samurai lies in the specialized roles of its members. Each card contributes to a complex network of searching, summoning, and control.

The Vanguards (Starters)

Battle Shogun of the Six Samurai

The premier modern starter. On Link Summon, discards 1 card to search for Gateway of the Six.

Shien's Smoke Signal

Searches any Level 3 or lower "Six Samurai" monster, providing access to starters like Kageki or tuners.

The Reinforcements (Extenders)

Legendary Six Samurai - Kizan

The most important extender. Special Summons itself from the hand if you control another "Six Samurai". Not once per turn.

Asceticism of the Six Samurai

A versatile Quick-Play Spell that Special Summons a monster from the deck with the same ATK as a targeted "Six Samurai".

Competitive Analysis

Strengths

  • Explosive Ceiling: Capable of creating game-winning boards, hand loops, or FTKs.
  • High Consistency: Numerous in-archetype and generic searchers make accessing starters reliable.
  • Resilience to Maxx "C": Can often "win the challenge" by decking out the opponent.

Weaknesses

  • Glass Cannon: Extremely fragile and vulnerable to a single, well-timed hand trap.
  • Poor Going-Second: Struggles to break established boards without specific side deck cards.
  • Choke Points: Key monsters like Isolde and Battle Shogun are major points of failure if negated.

Synergistic Engines

  • Isolde, Two Tales: The premier generic Warrior support, turning any two warriors into a full combo starter.
  • True Draco Alliance: Uses Dragonic Diagram to destroy Fuma for a search and a Special Summon.
