Amazoness

A Strategic Deep Dive into the Warrior Tribe

Executive Summary

The Amazoness archetype is a historic, battle-focused strategy centered on EARTH Warrior and Beast monsters. Its modern identity has evolved into a sophisticated, Fusion-based control deck that systematically dismantles an opponent's board during the Battle Phase. Rather than relying on a wall of negations, Amazoness employs an offensive-defensive posture, punishing opponents for engaging in combat through powerful trap effects and resilient boss monsters. The goal is to establish board supremacy and deliver a decisive victory through overwhelming battle presence.

Core Combo: The Princess Line

The deck's most reliable combo starts with a single card, Amazoness Princess, to build a formidable end board.

Amazoness Princess
1

Summon and Search

Normal Summon Amazoness Princess. Her effect activates, adding Amazoness Call from your deck to your hand.

Amazoness Spiritualist
2

Extend and Enable

Activate Amazoness Call to search for Amazoness Spiritualist. Use extenders like Amazoness Baby Tiger to summon Spiritualist, who then searches for Polymerization.

Amazoness Empress
3

Fusion Summon

Use Polymerization to fuse Princess and Spiritualist into Amazoness Empress. Her effect then summons Amazoness Queen from the deck, establishing the core lock.

Final Endboard: The Standard Control Board

The ideal first-turn setup creates a near-impenetrable defensive wall during the Battle Phase, punishing any opponent who dares to attack.

Amazoness Empress & Queen
Amazoness Onslaught
  • Amazoness Empress: Provides blanket protection from destruction by battle and card effects to all other "Amazoness" cards.
  • Amazoness Queen: Further protects your monsters from being destroyed by battle.
  • Amazoness Onslaught (Set): The key disruption piece. This Continuous Trap banishes any monster that battles an "Amazoness" monster, making attacks incredibly risky for the opponent.

Competitive Analysis

Strengths

  • Battle Phase Control: Excels at manipulating combat to banish and remove threats.
  • Resilient Board: The combination of Empress and Queen makes the field very difficult to clear via battle.
  • Swarm Capability: Can easily summon multiple monsters to fuel Fusion and Link plays.

Weaknesses

  • Linear & Fragile: The main combo is very dependent on the Normal Summon of Princess, which is easily disrupted by hand traps.
  • Weak Going First: Can struggle to establish a board with meaningful non-battle interaction for the opponent's first turn.

Synergistic Engines & Support

  • Diabellstar Engine: Provides a powerful, multi-card starter that can search for fusion spells and add resilience.
  • Generic Warrior Support: Cards like Reinforcement of the Army and Fusion Deployment improve consistency.

The Amazoness Arsenal

A detailed overview of the key warriors and tools of the tribe.

Amazoness Princess

The deck's primary starter and consistency engine. Searches any "Amazoness" Spell/Trap on summon.

Amazoness Call

A Quick-Play Spell that acts as a universal searcher for any "Amazoness" card and provides a battle-extending GY effect.

Amazoness Onslaught

A powerful Continuous Trap that banishes any monster that battles an "Amazoness" monster, turning the Battle Phase into removal.

Amazoness Empress

The main Fusion boss monster. Protects all "Amazoness" cards from destruction by battle or card effects.

Amazoness Spiritualist

An extender and Fusion enabler that can summon itself and search for "Polymerization".

Amazoness Baby Tiger

A free extender that Special Summons itself from the hand or GY when another "Amazoness" is summoned, providing easy material for plays.
