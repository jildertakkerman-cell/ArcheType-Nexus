Rescue-ACE

A Tactical Deep Dive into the FIRE-Attribute Archetype

Executive Summary

The Rescue-ACE (R-ACE) archetype is a FIRE-Attribute strategy designed around emergency firefighters and their high-tech vehicles. It operates as a mid-range control deck, prioritizing board presence and resource management to outlast the opponent. The deck's primary goal is to summon its boss monster, Rescue-ACE Turbulence, which sets up to four powerful and searchable "Rescue-ACE" Spells and Traps from the deck, creating a formidable wall of disruption. The entire strategy hinges on a simple yet powerful directive: RESCUE!, ALERT!, CONTAIN!, and EXTINGUISH! any and all threats.

The R-ACE Doctrine

Backrow-Based Control

The deck's win condition is built on the massive card advantage generated by Turbulence. By setting four distinct interruptions, the deck can control the tempo of the duel and systematically dismantle the opponent's strategy.

The Hydrant Dependency

The deck's power level skyrockets with Rescue-ACE Hydrant on the field. It unlocks more powerful secondary effects for all "Rescue-ACE" Spells/Traps and, crucially, allows them to be activated the turn they are set by Turbulence.

The Chain of Command: Core Roster

Rescue-ACE Hydrant

The undisputed heart of the deck. Searches any R-ACE monster and enables the entire backrow strategy.

Rescue-ACE Air Lifter

The primary one-card starter. Summons to search for EMERGENCY!, beginning the main combo line.

Rescue-ACE Turbulence

The main boss monster. Easy to summon and generates four interruptions from the deck in a single move.

Rescue-ACE Preventer

A free extender, non-destruction removal, and a floating effect all in one card. A pivotal tool for resilience.

Rescue-ACE Impulse

A powerful hand trap that allows "Turn 0" interaction by summoning a machine R-ACE from the deck.

EMERGENCY!

The archetypal "Emergency Teleport." Special Summons any R-ACE from the deck and helps play through disruption.

The Final Formation: Optimal End Board

A strong Rescue-ACE end board layers the archetype's backrow with powerful generic monsters from the Extra Deck, creating a multi-faceted wall of disruption.

On-Field Presence:

  • Turbulence & Hydrant: The core engine.
  • I:P Masquerena: For reactive Link Summons.
  • 4 Set S/T: Your primary interaction (RESCUE!, ALERT!, CONTAIN!, EXTINGUISH!).

Graveyard/Banished Setup:

  • Promethean Princess: GY effect for revival and destruction.
  • R-ACE cards: Fuel for HQ's draw effect and Preventer summons.

TCG Banlist Status

The Rescue-ACE core remains at full power, but key external engines that pushed it to Tier 0 status have been hit hard.

Unrestricted Core

  • All "Rescue-ACE" Main & Extra Deck monsters.
  • All "Rescue-ACE" Spell/Trap support cards.

Engine Hits

  • Original Sinful Spoils - Snake-Eye (Forbidden): The most powerful one-card starter for the deck is no longer playable.

Meta Implications: While the pure version of the deck is fully intact and consistent, the ban of "Original Sinful Spoils" forces the deck to rely more on its own starters like Air Lifter, making it slightly more vulnerable to a single point of disruption.
