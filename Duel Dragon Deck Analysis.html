THE MANGA'S DUEL DRAGONS

A Deep Dive into a Thematic Legacy

Introduction: Defining the "Duel Dragons"

The term "Duel Dragon" originates from the *Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's* manga, where it refers to a specific group of powerful Synchro Monsters. It's important to note this is not a mechanically cohesive, playable archetype. Instead, these dragons serve as the manga's narrative counterparts to the famous "Signer Dragons" from the anime series, linked by lore rather than gameplay. Each is a standalone boss monster designed to be the pinnacle of a character's strategy. This analysis will explore these individual powerhouses.
