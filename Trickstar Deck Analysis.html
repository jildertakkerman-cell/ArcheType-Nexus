TRICKSTAR

The Idols of Burn and Disruption

Executive Summary

The Trickstar archetype is a collective of LIGHT Fairy monsters with a vibrant, pop-idol aesthetic that conceals a relentlessly punishing playstyle. It operates as a multifaceted control and combo strategy, weaponizing small, incremental instances of effect damage to fuel an engine of disruption, resource advantage, and powerful Link Summons. Trickstar's core gameplay revolves around taxing the opponent for nearly every fundamental action they take, putting them on a strict clock while you assemble your own dazzling performance.

The Main Act: Core Engine

The Starters & Extenders

Trickstar Candina

The premier Normal Summon. Searches any "Trickstar" card from Deck to hand, providing one-card access to your entire strategy.

Trickstar Corobane

A free Special Summon from the hand if you control a "Trickstar" monster, enabling instant Link-2 plays.

Trickstar Lycoris

A key extender that Special Summons itself from hand by bouncing another Trickstar, allowing you to re-use Candina's search effect.

Backstage Crew: Support

Trickstar Light Stage

The Field Spell that acts as three more copies of your starter, searching any "Trickstar" monster on activation.

Trickstar Reincarnation

The infamous Trap card. Banishes the opponent's entire hand and allows them to draw a new one, setting up devastating hand-loop combos.

Dark Room of Nightmare

A Continuous Spell that adds 300 damage every time your opponent takes effect damage, drastically accelerating the burn strategy.

The Opening Sequence

The deck's consistency is demonstrated by its simple one-card starter combo, which converts a single Normal Summon into multiple on-field threats and disruptions.

Trickstar Candina
1

Summon & Search

Normal Summon Trickstar Candina. Use her effect to search for Trickstar Corobane.

Trickstar Corobane
2

Extend & Link

Activate Corobane's effect from your hand to Special Summon it. Use Candina and Corobane to Link Summon a Link-2 monster.

Trickstar Noble Angel
3

Establish Disruption

Summon Trickstar Noble Angel. Its effect can search Maiden of Blue Tears, a Trap that destroys an opponent's monster and triggers Noble Angel to destroy another card.

Competitive Analysis

Strengths

  • High Consistency: Multiple one-card starters make its opening plays extremely reliable.
  • Blowout Potential: The Reincarnation + Droll combo can win the game on turn one by banishing the opponent's entire hand.
  • Punishing Loop: The constant burn damage forces opponents to play cautiously and punishes search-heavy decks.

Weaknesses

  • Normal Summon Reliant: Very vulnerable to a single hand trap like Ash Blossom on Candina's search effect.
  • Low Individual Power: Outside of its key combos, individual monsters are weak and struggle to mount a comeback.
  • Predictable Choke Points: Experienced opponents know exactly where to apply disruption to stop the deck's main combo.

Key Tech Choices

  • Droll & Lock Bird: Essential for the hand-loop combo with Trickstar Reincarnation.
  • Called by the Grave: Crucial for protecting your Candina from hand traps like Ash Blossom.
  • Chain Summoning: Allows for multiple Normal Summons, enabling loops with Lycoris to search several cards in one turn.

Current TCG Banlist Status

The Trickstar archetype has seen significant impact from the banlist, curbing the power of its most oppressive strategies.
